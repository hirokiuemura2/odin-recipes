This is a recipe for healthy green juice, for anyone who's thirsty and wants a good drink quickly.


Ingredients:

  • 6 leaves kale
  • 4 stalks celery, leaves removed
  • 2 green apples, halved
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/2 lemon, peeled
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger

Directions:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Process kale, celery, green apples, cucumber, lemon, and ginger through a juicer.
  3. Serve immediately or store in a glass jar in the fridge for up to a day; shake before drinking.
